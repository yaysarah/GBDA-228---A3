About Me

Hello! I am Sarah Zhao, a University of Waterloo student. I am passionate about aviation, and am excited to share with you about aviation here!!

Hobbies - Flight Tracking and Plane Seeking

I like to do flight tracking and plane seeking in my free time. Along with flight tracking, I listen to ATC Departure and Arrivals of certain airports to listen to conversations between airline pilots and ATC. Sometimes, I also go plane seeking at airports.

Experience - Discovery Flight and Airline Flights

I flew in a grey Cirrus SR20 to do a discovery flight along the CN Tower in downtown Toronto, and the experience was breathtaking! Other than that, I have experienced airline flights too.
